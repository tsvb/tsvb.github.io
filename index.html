Mid Century Modern Architecture and Design

Mid Century Modern architecture and design emerged in the 1930s and flourished until the 1960s. This style is characterized by clean lines, organic shapes, and an emphasis on functionality.

Characteristics of Mid Century Modern Design

  • Large windows and sliding glass doors
  • Open floor plans
  • Minimal ornamentation
  • Use of natural materials
  • Integration with nature

Influential Architects of Mid Century Modern Design

  • Richard Neutra
  • Albert Frey
  • Donald Wexler
  • Charles and Ray Eames
  • Eero Saarinen

Design Principles of Mid Century Modern Architecture

  • Functionality and simplicity
  • Integration with nature
  • Use of natural light
  • Minimal ornamentation
  • Emphasis on horizontal lines
